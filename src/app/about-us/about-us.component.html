Realtime Queue Management System info
Using Realtime queue management system doctor and patients can save thier time by watching their realtime status in a Queue
  • Doctor can schedule his/her queue by setting it's attributes
  • Doctor can schedule any number of queues for his/her clinic/hospital and can manage all of them concurrently
  • Patients can book thier appointment based on state of the queue (live/booking/scheduled) and can get their appointment number assigned to them
