Booking Cancellation Policy

This section is applicable to all

  • Once patient successfully booked his/her booking in Doctor's queue, he/she can cancel this booking at any point of time if consulting does not happend
  • There are two type of cancellations :
    1. 1. Cancelled by Practitioner/Doctor
      • Practitioner/Doctor can cancel booking befor consultation time started.
      • Practitioner/Doctor can cancel booking after consultation time is over and consulting could not happened
      • If Practitioner/Doctor cancels a meeting at any time, fees paid by the patient will be refunded by Doctormeetup directly.

      2. Cancelled by Patient
      • Patient can cancel booking befor consultation time started.
      • Patient can cancel booking after consultation time is over and consulting could not happened
      • If a patient cancels a meeting, fees paid by the patient will be refunded by Doctormeetup directly if it is cancelled after consultation time is over and consulting could not happened
  • If cancellation is being done by the patient because of Doctor's fault it will be handled by Doctormeetup directly otherwise it is subject to Doctor's approval and upon doctor's approval Doctormeetup will process the same

Meeting Reschedule Policy

This section is applicable to all

  • Once patient successfully booked his/her booking in Doctor's queue, he/she can reschedule this meeting at any point of time if consulting does not happend
  • Patient can request Doctor to rechedule his/her particular meeting to any other date with a valid reason
  • once Practitioner/Doctor recieves this request he/she can approve or reject that reshedule request, Practitioner/Doctor is not bound to approve this request
  • If Practitioner/Doctor approves reschedule request Patient can book another free booking on that rescheduled date with his/her old booking reference Id
  • If Practitioner/Doctor rejects reschedule request, Patient can continue to this join ths meeting or skip that
  • In this case Practitioner/Doctor or Doctormeetup are not bound for any refund. But Practitioner/Doctor can refund this fees as per his/her choice/li>

Refund Policy

This section is applicable to all

  • Once patient successfully booked his/her booking in Doctor's queue, he/she can cancel this meeting at any point of time if consulting does not happend
  • Meetings will be cancelled in two ways:

      Cancelled by Practitioner/Doctor
      • If meeting is cancelled by Practitioner/Doctor then, patient's full refund of paid fees will be processed by Doctormeetup directly

      Cancelled by Patient
      • If meeting is cancelled by patient if Practitioner/Doctor did not contacted or conducted meeting then refund will be processed directly by Doctormeetup after full investigation.
      • If Practitioner/Doctor tried to contact Patient to conduct meeting but Patient could not join the meeting becase of any reason, in this case if Patient cancels the meeting then refund is subject to Practitioner/Doctor's approval.
      • If Practitioner/Doctor tried to contact Patient to conduct meeting but Patient could not join the meeting becase of any reason and Practitioner/Doctor approves Patient's cancellation request then your full refund will be processed by Doctormeetup
      • If Practitioner/Doctor tried to contact Patient to conduct meeting but Patient could not join the meeting becase of any reason and Practitioner/Doctor and Practitioner/Doctor rejects Patient's cancellation request then it will be considered as "no show" and refund will not be processed
      • If Practitioner/Doctor tried to contact Patient to conduct meeting but Patient could not join the meeting becase of any reason then Practitioner/Doctor has to approve or reject his/her cancellation request within 24 hours otherwise it will be considered as "no show" and refund will not be processed
  • All the refunds processed by doctor meetup will be credited to the same payment method which user used when making payment Doctormeetup will not deduct any cancellation fees hence user's full payment will be refunded
  • All refunds will be processed within 7 working days by Doctormeetup. If cancellation is subject to Practitioner/Doctor's approval than refund time time will be considered once Practitioner/Doctor approves the cancellation request
