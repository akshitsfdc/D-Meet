Queue creation and consulting fees declaration policy

This section is applicable to Practitioners

  • Practitioner has to be authenticated with his/her credentials
  • Practitioner should go to create queue and add it's attributes like fees, queue timings and average time per patient processing
  • Doctormeetup is not responsible for any dealy if Practitioner could not process all his/her patients in time.
  • Practitioner should declare his/her consulting fees while creating the queue.
  • Doctormeetup will add his service charge to the fees declared by the Practitioner, which will be shown to Practitioner and he/she is fee to change consulting fees based on that
  • After adding service charge the final fees will be show to the patients and he/she has to pay that charge while booking the queue
  • Doctormeetup will pay cunsulting fees of Practitioner after deducting service charge
  • Doctormeetup has all rights to change service charge with giving prior information to the Practitioner
  • The changed service charge will not be applied on the bookings which has been booked before service charge changed

Queue booking policy

This section is applicable to all

  • Based on the timings and patient limit of queue, any authenticated user can book the queue for doctor consultation
  • User has to pay consulting fees if Doctor does not allow offline payment
  • Doctormeetup is not responsible for any delay made by the doctor in processing his/her queue
  • Doctormeetup will show you your approximate waiting time based on average time per patient doctor takes.
  • Live queue will be visible to patients but if there is any issues in their internet connection and they are not able to see their live status, Doctor meetup is not responsible of this situation
  • After your online meeting with Practitioner you will be getting online prescription from Practitioner not from Doctormeetup
  • In case Practitioner does not write any Prescription to Patient, Doctormeetup will not be responsible for that
  • Validity of your prescription will be decided by the Practitioner(by validity we mean one can book free queue booking in refrence to his/her previous booking)
  • Practitioners are not bound to provide a validity on prescription it is their own decision.
